Privacy Policy of the affiliatesphere.org Website

At affiliatesphere.org, the privacy of our users and affiliate partners is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect data related to affiliate activities. If you need further information or have questions about this policy, please contact us directly.

Log Data Collection

affiliatesphere.org follows legal and secure practices for collecting log data. When affiliates interact with our platform, log files may record session details including IP addresses, browser types, ISP data, time and date, landing/referral URLs, and interaction clicks. This data is never linked to personally identifiable information and is solely used to improve tracking accuracy, campaign optimization, and to offer the best affiliate performance tools.

Advertising and Tracking

Our platform partners with top-rated ad networks. These advertisers may use cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons for tracking affiliate activity. These technologies automatically collect user IP addresses to assess campaign performance, improve ad relevancy, and verify affiliate referrals. affiliatesphere.org does not control the tracking methods of third-party advertisers but ensures that our systems operate within safe and legal boundaries.

Third-Party Policies

This privacy policy is specific to affiliatesphere.org and does not apply to other platforms or external services. For best practices, we recommend that affiliates review the privacy policies of any third-party advertising networks they engage with. You may also manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings to control tracking in a safe and legal way.

Affiliate Protection

We prioritize creating a secure environment for affiliate marketing. All collected data is used to ensure rating integrity, track performance fairly, and prevent abuse. affiliatesphere.org does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals who are not involved in affiliate activities.

Digital Policy Scope

This policy applies exclusively to online interactions and the digital environment of affiliatesphere.org. Any information shared offline or through unrelated methods is not covered under this policy.

Consent
