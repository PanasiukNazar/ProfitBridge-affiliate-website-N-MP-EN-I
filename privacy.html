Cookie Policy

Welcome to our affiliate platform!

To ensure the best experience for our affiliate partners and to make navigation smooth and effective, we use cookies. This policy explains what cookies are, how we apply them in the affiliate context, and your options for managing their use.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device (such as a laptop, mobile, or tablet) when you access a website. These files allow the site to remember your actions, session preferences, and affiliate tracking data to ensure a legal and efficient environment for all participants.

How do we use cookies?

We utilize cookies exclusively for affiliate-related purposes:

* Functionality: Cookies support account access and affiliate dashboard stability.
* Personalization: Cookies save your preferred affiliate settings to offer a personalized and secure experience.
* Analytics: We collect anonymous data to analyze affiliate performance, campaign effectiveness, and site traffic. This helps us provide new tools and optimize top-rated features.
* Marketing: Cookies assist in tracking affiliate links and verifying referrals, maintaining a fair and legal affiliate program structure.

What types of cookies do we use?

These cookie types are used to maintain a safe and high-rated affiliate program:

* Necessary cookies: These are essential for tracking referrals and affiliate login functionality.
* Functional cookies: Help remember your affiliate settings and preferences.
* Analytical cookies: Collect anonymized statistics to improve the affiliate system and tools.
* Marketing cookies: Used for evaluating campaign conversions and maintaining a reliable, rating-based structure.

How can I manage cookies?

You have the option to manage cookie settings for your affiliate experience:

* You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
* You may clear previously saved cookies from your device.
* Note that disabling cookies may limit your access to the full functionality of the affiliate platform and safe reporting tools.

For more details on how cookies help us maintain the best affiliate performance and safe data tracking, feel free to contact our support team.
